Zoe Margaret Colletti

Zoe Margaret Colletti, who plays Stella Nicholls in the movie Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark, is a young actress who played Tessie in the 2014 film version of Annie, and performed on the songs "Maybe" and "It's The Hard Knock Life" for the film's soundtrack. Before her fame, she was in a television movie called American Men in the role of Emma Wilson and she has also booked a recurring guest role on the 2010 season of the show Rubicon.

Austin Zajur, who plays Chuck Steinberg in the movie Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark, first become known when he played Carter in the 2016 film Delinquent, then joined the cast of the 2017 comedy Fist Fight. Before his fame, his first acting appearances were small guest roles in the episodes of the 2008 mini-series John Adams, and has had previous guest roles on a variety of popular television shows including The Carrie Diaries, TURN and Mercy Street.
